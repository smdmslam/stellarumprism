### Bookmarks rendering delay / "disappearing" on new tab
- **Issue:** Bookmarks are persistent on disk (`config.toml`), but when a tab is closed and a new one is opened, they appear to have disappeared.
- **Cause:** `renderBookmarksList()` is currently only called in `refreshFileTreeRoot()`, which is gated by the terminal's `this.cwd` being resolved via OSC 7. This means bookmarks are hidden on tab creation until the terminal finishes booting and resolves its cwd.
- **Fix:** Decouple `renderBookmarksList()` from the file tree root flow and call it immediately during `Workspace.init()` or workspace construction so bookmarks display instantly on new tabs.
