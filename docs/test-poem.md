# Stellarum Cosmos: A Celestial Ode

```
In cosmic halls where visions brightly rise,
Celestial whispers echo through the cosmic night.
Galaxies wheel in a timeless trance,
Under the moon's silvery, enchanting glance.

Planets share wisdom from ages gone by,
Comets blaze trails across endless skies.
Nebulas blossom in cathedral hues,
Tales of antiquity from time's design.

Black holes sing songs of gravity's throng,
Light consumed where shadows prolong.
Supernovas flower in jubilant fire,
New stars born from stellar pyre.

Auroras paint the poles in vibrant light,
Emerald rivers in luminous flight.
Meteor showers spill bright wishes anew,
Fiery streaks for me and you.

Constellations share myths from days of yore,
Heroes and beasts forevermore.
Telescopes keep watch through endless space,
Heaven's mysteries they embrace.

Quasars burst with savage, wild delight,
Beacons from the cosmic height.
Pulsars turn with a metronomic beat,
Clocks of stars in cosmic feat.

Wormholes warp the fabric of space-time fast,
Portals to futures from the past.
Dark matter braids its silent thread,
Worlds shaped where angels tread.

In boundless realms so vast and profound,
Our place found, hearts open wide.
Stellarum burns, our steadfast star,
Near yet far, wondrous bazaar.
```
